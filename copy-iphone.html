Copy to iPhone

Download signed Copy to iPhone.lbaction

Action that uses the Command-C application to send either the text or files selected in LaunchBar or your system clipboard to your iPhone. Requires that you have Command-C installed on your Mac and your iPhone.

Note: if you don't use iPhone as the device name for your phone in Command-C then after running this action once go to ~/Library/Application Support/LaunchBar/Action Support/com.renaghan.launchbar.CopyToIPhone/Preferences.plist and adjust the setting to match your device name
